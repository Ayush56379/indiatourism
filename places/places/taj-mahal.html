Taj Mahal

State: Uttar Pradesh

About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous monuments in India. It was built in 1632 by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time to visit Taj Mahal.

Entry Fee

Indian: ₹50 | Foreigner: ₹1100
